Paper Presentation

Winning Prizes: 1000/-, Medals, Certificates, and Gift hamper
Participation: Individual
Rules:

  • The theme of the presentation is ________.

  • Each team shall consist of one participant only.
  • Time duration : 5 min ( 4 min for presentation + 1 min for discussion )
  • Participants must submit a hard copy of their presentation to the judges before the presentation.

Mathematical Rangoli

Winning Prizes: 500/-, Medals, Certificates, and Gift hamper
Participation: Team
Rules:

  • The theme of the rangoli is any mathematical concept.
  • At most two teams (UG or PG) per college are permitted.

  • The time duration for the competition is one hour.
  • Participants should bring all necessary materials required for the rangoli.
