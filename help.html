FAQ

  • Will food be provided?
    Yes, food will be provided with a basic fare.
  • Do we get free accommodation?
    Limited hostel accommodation is available for an extra charge of 150 rupees. It is allocated on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • If we miss our online registration, can we register on the spot?
    On-spot registration may be available, but it will be subject to availability. Please check with the event coordinators in advance.
  • Will you provide the props required for events like the Rubik's cube competition?
    Yes, the necessary props will be provided for the events.
  • Is there a specific format for PowerPoint (PPT) presentations?
    Yes, PowerPoint presentations should follow the prescribed format. More details regarding the template, time limits, and submission process will be shared with registered participants.
  • Can students from different departments participate?
    Yes, students from all departments are welcome to participate. However, the talks and paper presentation competition will be subject-specific.
  • Can students from different colleges form a team?
    Yes, inter-college teams are allowed.
  • What happens if I arrive late?
    Late entry will be allowed until 10:30 AM. For lectures, you must inform one of the coordinators in advance.
  • Whom should I contact for more information?
    You can contact the event coordinators. Their contact details will be provided in the event brochure.
  • Do we need to follow a specific dress code?
    There is no fixed dress code; however, participants are expected to maintain proper decorum.
  • Are there any participation certificates?
    Yes,certificates will be provided to the winners of each event
  • Will there be any travel reimbursement?
    No, travel expenses will not be reimbursed.
  • Is there a limit to the number of events I can participate in?
    Some events may have participation limits. Please refer to the event guidelines for more details.

How To Reach NIT?

The National Institute of Technology (NIT) Trichy is located on the Trichy-Tanjore Highway (NH 67), approximately 20 kilometers from the main city of Tiruchirappalli (Trichy). The campus is well-connected by various modes of transport, making it accessible for students and visitors. Below are the available travel options:

  • By Bus:
    • You can take Bus No. 128 from the Central Bus Stand (CBS) to reach NIT Trichy.
    • Alternatively, you can board any Thanjavur-bound bus from CBS, as they pass through the NIT stop.
    • The journey typically takes about 40 minutes, depending on traffic conditions.
  • By Train:
    • The nearest railway station is Tiruchirappalli Junction (TPJ), which is well connected to major cities.
    • From the station, you can hire a taxi or take a local bus to NIT Trichy.
  • By Auto-Rickshaw or Taxi:
    • Auto-rickshaws are available near CBS and the railway station. It is advisable to negotiate the fare beforehand.
    • Taxi services, including ride-hailing options like Ola and Uber, are also available for a more comfortable journey.
  • By Air:
    • The nearest airport is Tiruchirappalli International Airport (TRZ), located about 25 km from NIT.
    • From the airport, taxis and app-based cab services are available for direct transport to the campus.

For further assistance, visitors can contact the NIT Trichy help desk or refer to local transport authorities for updated schedules and fares.
