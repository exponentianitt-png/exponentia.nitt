About Us

National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirapalli (NIT Trichy), is a prestigious institution celebrated for its contributions to science, research, and innovation. Since its establishment in 1964, it has been a hub for academic excellence, fostering a culture of inquiry and discovery.

about Department

Exponentia is the annual mathematics festival organized by the Mathematics Department of NIT Trichy. Bringing together students, educators, and math enthusiasts from across the country, Exponentia 2026 promises a series of engaging events, workshops, and competitions designed to celebrate the beauty and application of mathematics in various fields.
