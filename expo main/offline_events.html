Rubik's Cube

Entry Fees: 79/-
Winning Prizes: 500/-, Medals, Certificates, and Gift hamper
Participation: Individual
Rules:

  • Each round will have 4 participants competing to solve a Rubik's cube within 5 minutes.
  • Participants may bring their own rubik's cube.
  • The participant who completes the Rubik's cube correctly in the shortest time will be selected for the final round.
  • Multiple preliminary rounds will be conducted to select finalists.
  • The final round will have all selected participants competing to determine the overall winner.

Treasure Hunt

Entry Fees: 149/-
Winning Prizes: 1500/-, Medals, Certificates, and Gift hamper
Participation: Teams
Rules:

  • Each team will consist of three members.
  • Teams will receive the first clue at the start of the competition.
  • Using the first clue, teams must find the next clues step by step.
  • After finding each clue, teams must report to the committee to verify it before proceeding to the next round.
  • Only teams with the correct clue verification can continue to the next round.
  • The team that finds the final clue first will be declared the winner.
