Prof. C. Muthumariappan

Indian Institute of Astrophysics, Bangalore

Professor C. Muthumariappan is a Professor at the Indian Institute of Astrophysics (IIA) in Bengaluru, India, where he is part of the Galactic and Stellar Astronomy Group. He earned his Ph.D. and has a research portfolio that includes 33 publications, which have garnered 183 citations and 1,649 reads. His expertise encompasses areas such as infrared astronomy, optical design, interstellar medium studies, and stellar evolution.

Dr. N. Meenakshi Sundaram

Vivekananda College, Madurai

Dr. N. Meenakshisundaram is an associate Professor of Physics at Vivekananda College (Residential & Autonomous), Tiruvedakam West, Madurai. He has made significant contributions to research in Nonlinear Dynamics, Quantum Chaos, and Photonics and Material Discovery, actively engaging in these fields to advance scientific understanding. His academic work has been recognized in the research community, with 176 citations in scholarly literature, indicating the impact of his research.

About Us

National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirapalli (NIT Trichy), is a prestigious institution celebrated for its contributions to science, research, and innovation. Since its establishment in 1964, it has been a hub for academic excellence, fostering a culture of inquiry and discovery.

The Department of Physics at NIT Trichy is dedicated to advancing knowledge and promoting interdisciplinary research, it has produced notable advancements in astrophysics, optical sensors, and condensed matter physics. Modern labs are available, allowing students to investigate cutting-edge research subjects.

We conduct the national level event INPhyNITT every year to entice young minds to explore the Physics beyond the textbooks. The Department has been conducting the science fest ever since its inception. The name InPhyNITT was coined in 2012 and it lives on to this day. The event is a platform for students to showcase their talents and knowledge in the field of physics.

It includes a variety of events such as paper presentations, poster presentations, and quizzes. The event also features guest lectures by eminent scientists and researchers, providing students with an opportunity to interact with experts in the field. The event aims to foster a spirit of scientific inquiry and curiosity among students, inspiring them to pursue careers in physics and related fields.
